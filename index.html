About

Women represent less than 10% of programming, engineering, and developing jobs in the US Tech Industry. And under 5% of top executives and hiring managers are female.

The problem of under-representation and gender discrimination is rampant across the industry, as well as universities. Computer science and STEM professors have been scientifically show to demostrate bias and predjudice when interacting with female students.
